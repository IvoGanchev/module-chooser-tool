Welcome to Module Chooser Tool

Life is all about making choices. The right ones are always the hardest to make and taking the right path in your degree isn't always easy. Module Chooser Tool is here to bring that level of uncertanity down, and help students take the right modules during their degree. Your future is in your hands and with this tool you are able to shape your own degree path every day.

Main features of the Module Chooser Tool

  • Search and filter capabilities.
  • Checks if prerequisites are taken or not.
  • Shows future module choices based on the chosen module.
  • Calculates credits for each year of the programme
  • Intelligent notification system.
